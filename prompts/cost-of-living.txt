* **A. Average Rent (month)** — note if utilities are included.

* **D. **Total Monthly Cost**

* Give costs as: **value (local currency)**, **range (min–max)**, and **equivalent in USD** (with exchange rate and source).
* Provide a **table** with all scenarios and costs.
